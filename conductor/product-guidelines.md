# Product Guidelines

This document outlines the guidelines for the product's prose style, brand messaging, visual identity, tone of voice, and accessibility.

## Prose Style

The prose style should be **Informal and Engaging**. The goal is to make complex topics about AI accessible and interesting to a broad audience. The language should be conversational and approachable, avoiding overly academic or technical jargon where possible.

## Brand Messaging

The core brand message should revolve around the following themes:

*   **Empowerment and Education:** We empower people to understand and engage with AI.
*   **Innovation and Progress:** We are at the forefront of responsible AI development.
*   **Trust and Transparency:** We build trust through transparent and ethical AI.

## Visual Identity

The visual identity of the project should be **Minimalist and Clean**. This means a focus on simplicity, with a neutral color palette and generous use of white space. The design should be uncluttered and easy to navigate, allowing the content to be the primary focus.

## Tone of Voice

The tone of voice should be:

*   **Educational and Informative:** Aims to clarify complex topics and provide clear explanations.
*   **Empathetic and Approachable:** Focuses on connecting with the audience on a personal level.

## Accessibility

Accessibility is a key consideration for this project. The following principles should be applied:

*   **Clarity and Readability:** All content should be easy to understand and read for a broad audience. This includes using clear language, simple sentence structures, and legible typography.
*   **Inclusive Design:** The project should be designed for users with diverse needs and abilities. This includes considering color contrast, providing alternative text for images, and ensuring compatibility with screen readers.
